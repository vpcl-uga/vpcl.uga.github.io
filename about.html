The project focuses on creating detailed 3D reconstructions of coral structures by leveraging the Segment Anything Model version 2 (SAM 2) for image segmentation. By applying SAM 2 masks to high-resolution coral images, the project aims to isolate and extract coral formations from their backgrounds accurately. These precise masks are the foundation for building comprehensive 3D models of coral reefs. The resulting reconstructions will enable researchers and conservationists to visualize coral morphology better, monitor changes over time, and support efforts in coral health assessment and preservation of marine ecosystems.
