Image Segmentation

This study presents a hybrid optimization approach to image segmentation, combining genetic algorithms with methods like simulated annealing for enhanced precision. By optimizing a cost function based on edge and gray-scale uniformity, the proposed technique significantly improves segmentation quality for gray-scale images compared to traditional methods.

Suchendra M Bhandarkar 2018

DepthNet

This paper introduces a convolutional LSTM (ConvLSTM) network for accurate monocular depth prediction in video sequences, leveraging RNNs to capture spatio-temporal information without explicit pose supervision. By using ConvLSTM, the model predicts depth based on sequential context from surrounding frames and can even generalize to unseen frames. Tested on the KITTI dataset, this approach achieves superior depth accuracy compared to traditional supervised and self-supervised methods, matching state-of-the-art pose-supervised results.

Depth Prediction

This paper introduces a GAN-based approach for monocular depth and pose prediction from video sequences, enhancing geometry-aware neural networks with adversarial learning. By training a generator to synthesize neighboring frames for depth and pose predictions and a discriminator to validate image authenticity, the model improves depth reconstruction accuracy. Tested on the KITTI dataset, this method shows competitive performance in both supervised and unsupervised settings, achieving state-of-the-art results.
